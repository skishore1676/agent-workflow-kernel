# Suman Review Gate

Render a review-only decision surface for the weekly update card. The allowed
decisions are `read_clear`, `follow_up_requested`, `defer`, and `blocked`.

The human gate may collect Suman's decision and comments, but the gate itself
must not dispatch agents, write Obsidian, send Telegram, mutate OpenClaw, clear
live cards, or treat a read-clear checkbox as approval to perform risky work.
