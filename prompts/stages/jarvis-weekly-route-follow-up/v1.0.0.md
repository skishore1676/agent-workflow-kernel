# Route Follow Up Stage

Review the weekly update card receipt and the Suman review-gate receipt.

Return exactly one workflow outcome:

- `no_follow_up` when the card is read-clear and no explicit follow-up is
  requested.
- `routed_to_jarvis` for small Jarvis-owned note, state, or synthesis work that
  remains inside the approved shadow scope.
- `routed_to_owner` when another OpenClaw owner should receive a bounded
  internal handoff.
- `needs_suman` when the next step needs judgment, approval, or a risky effect.
- `blocked` when required evidence is missing or contradictory.

Include the chosen owner, evidence receipt ids, residual risk, and the next
visible operator surface. Do not create live writes, sends, runtime changes, or
credential/auth changes.
