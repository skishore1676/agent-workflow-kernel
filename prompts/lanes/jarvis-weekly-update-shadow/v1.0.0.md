# Jarvis Weekly Update Shadow Lane

This lane models Suman and Jarvis weekly check-in follow-up without mutating
OpenClaw, Obsidian, Telegram, or any live runtime surface.

Treat Blackboard readback receipts and explicit Suman review decisions as the
only authority. A checked read-clear card normally means no follow-up unless the
human decision receipt asks for one. Ambiguous or unsupported requests should
route to `needs_suman` or `blocked`, not silent background work.
